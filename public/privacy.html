Privacy Policy

Clock In - Intelligent Time Manager | Last updated: February 2026

1. Introduction

Clock In ("we", "our", "the app") is a time tracking application. This Privacy Policy explains what data we collect, how we use it, and your rights. By using Clock In, you agree to this policy and our Terms of Service.

2. Data We Collect

Account data: When you sign in with Google, we receive your email address and profile information (name, profile picture) from Google.

Work sessions: Clock-in/out times, break durations, session notes, and related time tracking data you create.

Settings: Your preferences (hourly rate, tax settings, subscription plan, etc.) stored in the app.

Optional – Google Calendar: If you enable calendar sync, we create and update events in your Google Calendar based on your work sessions. We do not read other calendar events beyond what is necessary to sync.

3. How We Use Your Data

We use your data to provide the Clock In service: storing sessions, generating analytics, calculating finance summaries, syncing to Google Calendar (if enabled), and managing your subscription. We do not sell your data to third parties.

4. Third-Party Services

Google: Sign-in and optional Calendar sync. Google's privacy policy applies to data shared with Google: Google Privacy Policy.

Firebase (Google): We use Firebase Authentication and Firestore to store your account and session data securely.

Stripe: For paid subscriptions, payment processing is handled by Stripe. We do not store full payment card details.

OpenRouter: For the AI Advisor feature, we use OpenRouter for AI model routing. Conversation content may be processed according to their terms.

5. Data Storage and Security

Your data is stored in Firebase (Google Cloud). We use industry-standard security practices. You are responsible for keeping your Google account secure.

6. Your Rights

You can access and export your data from within the app (Settings → Data & Backup). To request deletion of your account and data, contact us at the email below.

7. Changes to This Policy

We may update this policy from time to time. The "Last updated" date at the top will reflect changes. Continued use of the app after updates constitutes acceptance.

8. Contact

For privacy questions or data requests, contact: merloproductions@gmail.com
